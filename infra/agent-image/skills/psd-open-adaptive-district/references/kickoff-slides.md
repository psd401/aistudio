# The Open Adaptive *District*

Cycle 01 begins today. 90 minutes from now, every team has a draft Bet Brief in hand and the Logbook on their phone.

Slide 01· Why we are here

## A district that moves at the pace of *change.*

AI capability doubles every six months. District change cycles take years. That gap is where public education loses ground.

We are going to operate differently here — small cross-silo teams, six-week experiments, honest public debriefs, a quarterly intent that holds the line on what matters.

**This is the start.**

Slide 02· The Quarterly Intent

## Three sentences. No more.

**The intent for this quarter**

By the end of this quarter, we are after \[the outcome the superintendent names\].

This matters now because \[the moment we are in\].

We will not compromise on \[the non-negotiables\].

*Every team's bet lives inside this intent. The intent sets **what**; teams own **how.***

Slide 03· The SHIP cycle

## Six weeks active. Two weeks to plan. *Repeat.*

Phase 01

S

Sense

What changed? What problem are we trying to solve?

Phase 02

H

Hypothesize

If we do X, we expect Y. Here's how we'll know.

Phase 03

I

Implement

Run the bet. Stay close. Adjust as evidence comes in.

Phase 04

P

Publish

Adopt or kill. Say so publicly.

Slide 04· The three brief patterns

## Every cycle ends with one of *three.*

### Adoption Brief

It worked.

What we tried, what we learned, why we're scaling, what others should know.

### Decommission Brief

It didn't.

What we tried, what we learned, why we're stopping, what others should not repeat.

### Continuation Brief

We need another cycle.

What we tried, what we learned, what we're testing next, revised hypothesis.

*Decommissioning publicly is a **feature,** not a failure. Quiet abandonment is the failure mode.*

Slide 05· The Logbook

## An agent skill you load into *your* AI.

**The Logbook** reads your team's Google Chat Space, synthesizes across teams, coaches you through your cycle, and drafts your end-of-cycle brief.

It does four jobs:

- **Synthesize** — weekly digest across teams
- **Coach** — walk you through any SHIP phase
- **Draft** — first drafts of your briefs
- **Flag** — accountability and conflict surfacing
