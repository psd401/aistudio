# Procedure 3520P: Student Use of District Technology

## Purpose

This procedure explains how students may use district-provided devices and networks. It supports safe, responsible, and equitable access to technology for learning.

## Scope

The procedure applies to all students enrolled in the district who use district devices, the district network, or district accounts, whether on campus or remotely.

## Acceptable Use

Students use technology for instructional purposes. Students protect their account credentials and do not share passwords. Students report damaged or lost devices to their teacher promptly.

## Privacy and Monitoring

The district may monitor use of its network and devices to keep students safe. Students should not expect privacy when using district systems. Personal information is handled under district policy and applicable law.

## Consequences

Misuse of technology may lead to loss of access and other discipline consistent with district policy. Staff apply consequences fairly and consider the student's age and intent.
